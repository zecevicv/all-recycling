The old computer equipment, is mostly turned into electronic waste. This waste is highly toxic and dangerous, which also entails many costs for the customer - such as storage and management Inventory.

The metals present in the obsolete computing equipment thrown in the trash decompose rapidly and seep into groundwater. In Israel, there is a constant increase in the proportion of metals in drinking water.
